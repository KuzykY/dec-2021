The Simpson

American satirical animated series created by Matt Graining for Fox. The animated series is a satirical parody of the US middle class lifestyle, embodied in the Simpsons family of Homer, Marge, Bart, Lisa and Maggie. Most of the events take place in the fictional town of Springfield. The show ridicules many aspects of people's lives, American culture, society, and even American television.

Homer Simpson

Homer Jay Simpson-one of the main characters of the animated series "The Simpsons". Homer is a sweet and funny, but sometimes rude and impolite father of the family. He has obvious flaws: fat, bald and not very smart. He often behaves like a clown, absurd, selfish and tactless, but still remains likable. Homer has three children: Bart, Lisa and Maggie. Works as a safety inspector at the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant. Homer is reluctant to work and drinks a lot.

Marge Simpson

Marge Simpson-one of the main characters in the animated series The Simpsons, the wife of Homer Simpson, the mother of Lisa and Bart. A housewife, she spends most of her time at home caring for Maggie's youngest child, as well as Bart, Lisa and her husband.Marge is named after the mother of the creator of the series Meta Graining Margaret (Marge) Graining.

Charles Montgomery Burns

Charles Montgomery Burns- is the owner of the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant in the The Simpsons franchise. He is Springfield's richest, oldest, and most powerful citizen. His net worth has been stated to be in the billions and in one instance was pegged at exactly $1,800,037,022. He is one of the most richest and oldest men in Springfield.
